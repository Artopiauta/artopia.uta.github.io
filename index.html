About Us

Our Mission
Artopia, a creative healing program at the University of Texas at Arlington, brings together students, faculty, and the university and Dallas-Fort Worth communities to foster health and healing for people through therapeutic, educational, and expressive purposes.

Our Workshops
1

Workshop #1

Visual Meditation Making Mandalas
The circle format of the Mandala provided a visual container for wellness.
2

Workshop #2

Painting Your Future with Vision Boards
Participants designed their own personal vision board in this create-a-long process. They experimented with paint, collage, and a fun image transfer technique to complete their vision board. It served as a visual focusing tool for personal goals.
3

Workshop #3

Painting Challenges Away: Watercolor Weave
Explored the visual expression of life challenges and integrated with sources of personal strength.
4

Workshop #4

Resilience: The Art of the Cover-Up
Life happens, but our underlying strengths allow us to move forward. Participants created an oil pastel image that represented core values, and let them shine through the watercolor of life events.
5

Workshop #5

Technology, Movement & Art
Participants joined an art experience that bridged the use of technology, creative movement, and visual forms for relaxation, fun and using new technology like the leap motion device.
